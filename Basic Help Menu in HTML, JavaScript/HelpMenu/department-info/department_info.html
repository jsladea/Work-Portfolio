Department Overview

The "Department View" button can be found in the first section of the "Management Tools" toolbar in the management tab.


Instructions:

  1. Navigate to the Department Overview Page.


Information:

  • The first table ("Required Trainings Assigned to Employees") shows the required trainings that are assigned to employees in this department. You can double click a row in this table for more in-depth information.

  • The second table ("Trainings Assigned To Department") shows the trainings assigned to the department. These are the trainings that are automatically assigned to any employees when they are added to this department. This table shows trainings for the entire department (first row), and all child departments in the subsequent rows. It also shows you the members of each department and their job titles in the right-most column of the table.
