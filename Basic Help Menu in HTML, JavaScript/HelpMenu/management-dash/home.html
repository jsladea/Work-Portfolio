Management Dashboard Home

The Management Dashboard page can be accessed by navigating to the Management tab.


Instructions:

Using the Dashboard Homepage:


The dashboard has a table titled "Pending Tasks". In this table are listed the trainings completed by employees that are waiting for management approval. Approval tasks show up in this table when employees did not meet the minimum requirements for automatic approval of their trainings. If the employee took more than the allowed attempts to satisfactorily complete a training quiz, management approval will be required to complete the training process.



Approving Trainings:

  1. Double click the row in the table for the training that you wish to approve.
  2. Follow the onscreen prompts.
