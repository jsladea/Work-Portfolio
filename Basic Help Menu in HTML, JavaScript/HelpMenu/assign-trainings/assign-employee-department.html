Assign Training to Employees by Department

The Assign Training page can be found under the "Add" section of the Training Menu in the Management tab.

Assigning trainings to employees by department does not assign trainings to a department. It assigns the trainings to the selected employees of the selected departments. It is useful for assigning a training to a group of employees in a department(s) when the training will only need to be completed by those specific employees and not need to be assigned to new employees or employees added to that department.


Instructions:

  1. For the "Assign To" field, choose "Employee(s) by Department". A box will appear to the right of the "Employee(s) by Department" box. In this box you may select one or more departments. The employees from the selected departments will be automatically added to the "Selected Employees Box".
  2. Add Trainings by searching for the training and then pressing the "Add" button next to the training search box. Trainings can be unselected by clicking the checkmark next to the training in the "Selected Trainings" box.
  3. Specific employees can be unselected by clicking the checkmark next to their names in the "Selected Employees" box.
  4. Choose whether the training should be required for the selected employees.
  5. Choose a due date for the training. Due dates in the past are invalid (only current day-future dates may be selected).
  6. Press the "Assign Training" button on the bottom-right-hand side of the page.
